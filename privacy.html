Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

Cue ("we", "us", "our") operates the Cue family management service accessible via SMS and the web dashboard at hicue.ai. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including: names and phone numbers of family members, text messages sent to Cue, emails forwarded to your family's Cue email address, calendar events shared with Cue, and household schedule and budget information you share with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide the Cue service (schedule management, spending tracking, reminders), identify you when you text or email Cue, generate AI-powered responses to your questions, and improve the accuracy of our parsing and recommendations.

3. How We Protect Your Information

All data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database with Row Level Security enforced on every table. API keys and credentials are encrypted. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Cue: Twilio (SMS delivery), Anthropic (AI processing), Supabase (database hosting), and SendGrid (email processing). These services process data only as needed to provide their functionality and are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Cue does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Children's data is managed by their parent or guardian (the account admin). Parents can review, modify, or delete their children's information at any time through the Command Center or by texting Cue.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request a full export of your family's data or complete deletion of your account at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access all data we store about your family, correct any inaccurate information, export your data, and delete your account and all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via SMS or the Command Center dashboard.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@hicue.ai.
